# Bento Buddies

Bento Buddies is a web app designed to help UBC students connect with others on campus for spontaneous or planned meals. Whether you're looking to meet new people, try a new restaurant, or find someone to eat with between classes, Bento Buddies makes it easy to match with students nearby.

## Website Overview

When users log in to Bento Buddies, they are directed to the **Homepage**, which serves as the main hub of the app. From there, users can navigate through several key tabs:

- **Maps**: Explore popular food spots on campus and create a meetup
- **Events**: Check upcoming social meal events or group meetups organized by other students
- **Messages**: Chat with other users who have similar food interests and coordinate meal plans
- **Profile**: View and edit your personal information and profile
